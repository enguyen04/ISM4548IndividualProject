Meet HUNTR/X

HUNTR/X (헌트릭스) is a 3-member Korean girl group, that consists of Rumi, Mira, and Zoey.

Rumi (center) is the leader and main vocalist, Mira (right) is the main dancer and sub-vocalist, and Zoey (left) is the main rapper and sub-vocalist.

Meet the Saja Boys

Saja Boys (사자소년) is a 5-member Korean boy group, that consists of Jinu, Mystery, Abby, Baby, and Romance.

JINU (center) is the leader and main vocalist, MYSTERY (far left) is the main dancer and sub-vocalist, ABBY (middle left) is the main rapper and sub-vocalist, BABY (middle right) is the maknae and rapper, and ROMANCE (far right) is the main vocalist.
