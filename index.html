What is KPop Demon Hunters?

Its a movie about "A world-renowned K-Pop girl group balance their lives in the spotlight with their secret identities as demon hunters."

KPop Demon Hunters began streaming on Netflix on June 20, 2025, and became the most-watched original title in Netflix history with over 500 million views. It received acclaim for its animation, visual style, voice acting, story, and music.

It won Best Animated Feature and Best Original Song ("Golden") at the 83rd Golden Globe Awards and the 98th Academy Awards.
